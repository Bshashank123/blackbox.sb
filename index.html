Blackboard Game

Numbers 1–100 • First move must be an even number • Then move by ×prime or ÷prime

Player 1’s turn No number circled yet
Game Rules
  1. Numbers 1 to 100 are on the board.
  2. Player 1 starts by choosing any even number and circles it.
  3. On each subsequent move, choose a number that equals the currently circled number multiplied by a prime or divided by a prime (must remain an integer). The number must still be on the board.
  4. After choosing, the previously circled number is erased from the board, and the new one becomes circled.
  5. If you have no valid move on your turn, you lose.
